Crockpot Spaghetti

Crock Pot Spaghetti and Meatballs is an all-in-one meal! Perfect for a buffet-style meal or potluck! So easy and only a handful of ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 24 ounce jar spaghetti sauce
  • 20 ounce bag frozen meatballs
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Â½ (16 ounce) box spaghetti noodles
  • 2-3 Tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. In a 6-quart oval slow cooker, pour in half the jarred spaghetti sauce and do your best to spread it around evenly.
  2. Layer the frozen meatballs in a single layer (as best as possible) on top of spaghetti sauce.
  3. Pour the rest of the spaghetti sauce over the meatballs.
  4. Then pour in 4 cups of water.
  5. Sprinkle minced garlic, Italian seasoning, and basil on top. No need to stir.
  6. Finally, layer the spaghetti noodles on top and evenly drizzle the spaghetti noodles with 2-3 tablespoon olive oil to lightly coat.
  7. Then, gently push down the spaghetti noodles until they are under the water (breaking them if necessary to fit.)
  8. Try to make sure each noodle is coated in the sauce. This will help to keep the noodles from sticking together while cooking.
  9. Cover and cook on low for about 2.5 hours (until noodles are soft and meatballs are heated through).
  10. About halfway through, gently stir the noodles with a pasta fork to make sure none of the noodles are sticking together.
  11. Spaghetti is ready when the noodles are al dente and most of the liquid has been absorbed by the pasta.
